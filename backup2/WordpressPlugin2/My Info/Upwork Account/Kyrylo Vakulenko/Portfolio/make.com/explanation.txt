Title: Workflow Automation using make.com

content:
Slack-triggered data processing workflow:

1. Trigger from Slack – maybe a command, message, or reaction.

2. Retrieve rows from Google Sheets – probably multiple sheets or tabs.

3. Data cleaning & transformation – applying tools to normalize or extract specific fields.

4. Store results – write back to Google Sheets, possibly different sheets for each branch.

5. Send email – a final report, summary, or notification email.
